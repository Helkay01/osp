Cookie Policy

What cookies this site uses, and how to manage them.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function, remember preferences, and provide information to site owners about how the site is used.

2. Categories of cookies we use

Strictly necessary cookies — required for the site to function, such as remembering your cookie consent choice. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site by collecting information anonymously, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.

Advertising cookies — used by third-party ad networks, including Google, to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance. These may track your browsing activity across other sites.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our pages, including advertising and analytics providers. We do not control these cookies directly — please refer to the relevant third party's own cookie policy for details, such as Google's Cookie Policy.

4. Managing cookies

Most browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality. You can also opt out of personalized advertising cookies via Google Ads Settings or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact us

Questions about our use of cookies can be sent via our contact page.
